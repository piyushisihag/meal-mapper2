— About

Food is more than fuel.
It's an experience.

Meal Mapper was built for exactly that moment — it's 7pm, your stomach is growling, and you're staring at random ingredients with no idea what to cook.

We're a simple, smart recipe finder that takes the stress out of cooking. Tell us what's in your kitchen and we'll instantly map out every meal you can make, complete with step by step instructions. No fancy ingredients. No culinary skills required. Zero waste.
